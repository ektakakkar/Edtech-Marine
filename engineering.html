Engineering Services

Edtech Marine Services provides all types of vessels to repair.

  • Afloat repairs at Anchorage, OPL and in port
  • Repairs at Shipyard
  • Voyage repairs

Services

  • Steel Structural Repairs and Scaffolding.
  • Fabrication of Platforms and Ladders
  • Spooling Shafts and Spooling Tongue
  • Metal Spray on Shafts OD and ID of End Covers
  • Winch Shafts and Winch Drums
  • Gears (Helical, Spiral, Bevel and Sprockets)
  • Compressor and Pump overhauling.
  • Pipeline Renewals, Fabrication and Repairs.
  • Mechanical Repairs
  • Tank Cleaning and De-slopping/ Under-water hull cleaning and videography.
  • Boiler Repairs and Re-tubing.
  • Electrical Repairs and Motor Rewinding.
  • Reconditioning Hydraulic and Pneumatic Valves.
  • Repair and Servicing of Hydraulic Pumps and Motors Overhauling.
  • Winches and Windlass Repairs.
  • Cargo Cranes and Gantries Repairs.
  • Hydro Blasting and Tank Coatings
  • Winches and Windlass Repairs.
  • Fabrication and Installation of Anti-Piracy Measures.
  • Pressurized Vessel Repairs.

  • Turbo Charger Repair and Balancing.

Major Repairs, Fabrication & Conversions

Edtech Marine can arrange major repairs, modifications and conversions. Edtech Marine can provide engineers, supervisors and all types of repairs squad to undertake any type of voyage repairs / services on the vessel.

Superintendence Supervision

Edtech Marine can arrange for Engineering & Marine Superintendent to supervise & act as owner's representative for any shipping related activity.

Slop Disposal by Authorised Agencies

Government approved slop/Bilge water disposal can be arranged. Edtech Marine undertakes tank cleaning works & disposal of sludge.
