Singapore is the world’s busiest port where approximately 50,000 vessels pass through every year. Many owners and charterers find it convenient to receive spares and stores, carry out crew changes and receive Cash to Master (CTM) while passing through Singapore.

We at Edtech Marine can provide services required in the most timely and cost-effective manner without compromising safety of personnel and equipment. Some of the services we provide include:

  • Crew changes including changing visas and entry formalities
  • Cash to Master
  • Crew handling (meet & greet, hotel bookings, shore pass arrangements, etc.)
  • Crew welfare (doctor, dentist, mail, and prepaid telephone calling cards)
  • Inward & Outward clearance
  • Liaison with local authorities
  • Communication assistance

We have excellent relations with suppliers, port & immigration authorities in Singapore, ensuring that client’s needs are given
preference.
