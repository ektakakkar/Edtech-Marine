Edtech Marine was established with the purpose of single point professional services to all requirements of the marine industry including but not limited to supplies of stores and spares worldwide, inspection and servicing of lifesaving and firefighting equipment, repairs, storage services, agency and repair/fabrication services. At Edtech Marine, we ensure our products are reliable and cost-effective. We take pride in being a leading supplier of marine safety, life-saving and firefighting equipment in Singapore. Our safety products are priced at one of the best market prices in Singapore and are guaranteed of its reliability. Edtech Marine has established partnerships with some very well established manufacturers around the world lasting over 15 years now. We are honoured to be given the chance in representing them and strive to carry on these partnerships in the future. Our esteemed customers come from various industries and include shipping and industrial organisations such as ship Owners, ship managers, refineries, shipyards, factories, offshore vessels and industrial equipment manufacturers. Supported by a knowledgeable, dedicated and passionate sales and support team, Edtech Marine is able to provide expert knowledge, information and troubleshooting to help serve our customers. At Edtech, our highly qualified team of Engineers consist of a good mix of experience and youth allowing for quality services at all times.
