Safety & Automation

Life Saving Appliances Servicing :

Edtech Marine provides servicing of Life Saving Appliances (LSA), by Authorized representatives.

  1. Rescue Boat : Annual servicing, Repair & Replacement.
  2. Life Raft : Annual servicing, Repair & Replacement
  3. Life Boats : Annual servicing, upgrading, survey & load test.
  4. Immersion Suit (Annual Servicing/Repairs 3 yearly testing & replacement)
  5. Life Jackets : Servicing & replacement.
  6. SCBA : Hydrostatic test, Annual servicing, refilling & repair.
  7. EEBD, ELSA : Hydrostatic test, Annual servicing, refilling & repair.
  8. Oxygen Resuscitator (Hydrostatic test, Annual servicing, refilling & repair)
  9. BA Compressor : (Annual servicing, Repair & Replacement.)
  10. Air Quality Test (Annual Air Quality Analysis)
  11. Foam Compound : Annual Foam Analysis.

Fire Fighting Appliances Servicing :

  1. Galley Wet Chemical System:( Annual servicing, Repair & Replacement.)
  2. Co2 Systems HP system & LP system : Annual servicing, repairs, hydrostatic test. surveys & replacement & conversion from halon to Co2 system.
  3. FM 200 Systems : (Annual Servicing ,Repairs, Hydrostatic Test Surveys & Replacement)
  4. Sprinkler systems : Annual servicing, testing & survey
  5. Fire Extinguishers : All types of Extinguishers, Annual servicing, refilling, hydrostatic test, repairs & replacement.
  6. Dry Powder Systems (Annual servicing, repairs, hydrostatic Test. Surveys & replacement.)
  7. Fire Alarm Systems Servicing, testing, surveys .repairs & replacement.
  8. Foam Systems ( High & Low Expansion) : Annual servicing & testing
  9. Fire Pumps & Systems : Servicing, test & certification.

*We provide first filling & installation supervision for new acquisition.

* Pre-Survey Test : Edtech can carry out supervision & Pre Survey test for any of above installation & assist during survey.

Calibration & Servicing of Gas Monitoring Systems:

Edtech Marine provides Annual calibration of all Products related to Automation Engineering. All makes & types of gas monitors
can be Serviced, Repaired & Certified. Our Services also extend to all shipboard Automation and Service Repairs.

  4. Portable Tank Gauging System
  5. Engine Remote Control System
